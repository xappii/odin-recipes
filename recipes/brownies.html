Brownies

Description

This recipe from the Hummingbird Bakery makes very tasty brownies. Johannes adores these brownies.

The key to this recipe is patience. These brownies are slightly disappointing fresh, but taste better the day after they're cooked.


Ingredients

  • 200g dark chocolate
  • 175g unsalted butter
  • 325g caster sugar
  • 130g plain flour
  • 3 eggs
  • Chocolate chunks

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 170C. Grease the baking tray.
  2. Melt together the chocolate and butter.
  3. Stir in the sugar.
  4. Stir in the flour.
  5. Stir in the eggs.
  6. Transfer the batter to the baking tray.
  7. Press the chocolate chunks into the batter.
  8. Bake in the oven until flakey on top (approximately 30 minutes).
