Seasoned tofu

Description

This is the baked tofu recipe I have used for almost a year now.


Ingredients

  • Block of tofu
  • A load of soy sauce
  • 2 tsp garlic granules
  • 2 tsp onion granules

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 200C.
  2. Chop the tofu block into chunks.
  3. Mix together the soy sauce, garlic granules and onion granules.
  4. Toss the tofu chunks in the soy sauce mixture.
  5. Transfer the tofu chunks to a baking tray.
  6. Add the excess soy sauce mixture on top of the tofu chunks.
  7. Bake for 20 minutes.
