Chocolate cake

Description

Megan gave me this recipe. It is a Mrs Beeton recipe.


Ingredients

  • 200g plain flour
  • 300g caster sugar
  • 50g cocoa
  • 2 tsp bicarbonate of soda
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 100g butter
  • 200ml milk
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 180C. Grease the tin.
  2. Sift the dry ingredients into a mixing bowl.
  3. Add the butter and 175ml of the milk, mixing well until smooth.
  4. Add the remaining milk, eggs and vanilla extract.
  5. Transfer the batter to the prepared tin.
  6. Bake for 35-40 minutes.
